Unit 12: Module Review

This final unit focused on reflection, evaluation, and consolidation of learning from across the module. It provided opportunity to assess personal and professional development, identify areas for continued growth, and prepare the e-portfolio for submission.

Learning Outcomes

  • Evaluate personal learning journey across the module
  • Identify areas for continued professional development
  • Consolidate evidence of competency in a portfolio
  • Plan future learning activities based on module experience

Unit Reflection

Criteria Experience Learning
What?
(Description)		 In this unit, I reviewed the entire module journey and completed the e-portfolio preparation. I studied additional material on future directions in big data, including emerging technologies and evolving best practices in data management. The unit readings covered topics including data governance maturity models, emerging technologies in data processing, and the evolving landscape of data privacy regulations beyond GDPR.
So what?
(Analysis)		 Reviewing my work across all twelve units revealed significant growth in both technical skills and conceptual understanding. Areas that seemed challenging in early units now feel more manageable, demonstrating real learning progression. The module has transformed my understanding of data work from isolated technical tasks to interconnected strategic activities. I now see how extraction, cleaning, storage, compliance, and analysis form a coherent discipline requiring both technical and governance perspectives.
Now what?
(Outcome)		 I will continue developing skills identified as growth areas, particularly advanced Python programming and distributed data systems. The e-portfolio will serve as a foundation for ongoing professional documentation. Professionally, I will apply module learning immediately to projects at the Innovation Centre. I also plan to pursue additional certifications in data protection and database administration to formalise competencies developed through this module.
